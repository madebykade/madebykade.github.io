We are a collection of developers who make successful businesses

What happens when a group of talented programmers come together with a broad range of digital skills, and you give them a "basic income" to do whatever they like? That's what we do at Kade.
We are a company that focus on building real businesses and shipping great products to valuable markets. Everybody who is a part of the Kade Collective works on their own projects with full autonomy, while leveraging the collective skills, talent, and assets of the company.
